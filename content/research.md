Current writing and research projects:

Co-authoring upcoming book with Dr. Abel Bartley on the history of Springfield Baptist Church in Greenville, South Carolina

Co-authoring book chapter about the significance of Springfield Baptist Church in _The New Greenbook of South Carolina_ with Dr. Andrew Baker.

Ongoing work on notable Black citizens in the history of Greenville, South Carolina with emphasis on the years of 1877 to 1941. This work includes creating a new database, conducting a network analysis of findings, and using GIS to model where significant citizens lived and worked.
